Our Wildfire AI Solution

Why?

Wildfires are intensifying across Greece, threatening lives, homes, and ecosystems. Communities often lack localized, timely, and trustworthy alerts. Our mission is to close this gap.

What?

We are combining predictive wildfire modeling with community-level data to offer location-specific alerts, personalized prevention tips, and real-time evacuation guidance all visualized on an interactive AI-powered map.

How?

Using satellite imagery, weather forecasts, geospatial data, and local infrastructure details we deliver dynamic risk maps with simulated fire behavior, suggested evacuation routes, and matched community resources to people in need.
